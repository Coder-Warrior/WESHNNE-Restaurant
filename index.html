Our Recipes

1. Spaghetti Carbonara

Cook spaghetti. Fry pancetta with garlic. Mix with eggs, cheese, and pasta.

2. Chicken Tikka Masala

Marinate chicken in spices. Grill and add to tomato and cream sauce. Simmer and serve with cilantro.

3. Sushi Rolls

Prepare sushi rice. Place on nori, add fillings, and roll tightly. Serve with wasabi and soy sauce.

4. Margherita Pizza

Top pizza dough with tomato sauce, mozzarella, and basil. Bake until crust is golden. Drizzle with olive oil.

RestaurantOwner

Ahmed Fawzy

Ahmed Fawzy is the visionary founder and owner of WESHNNE, a culinary gem in the heart of Cairo. His journey in the culinary world began at a young age, inspired by his family's rich traditions and his grandmother's recipes. This early exposure ignited a passion for cooking that led him to pursue a degree in Culinary Arts from the prestigious Le Cordon Bleu in Paris.

After graduating, Ahmed spent several years honing his skills in some of the world's finest kitchens, working alongside renowned chefs and gaining invaluable experience. In 2015, he returned to Cairo with a vision to create a unique dining experience that blends traditional Egyptian flavors with modern culinary techniques.

WESHNNE quickly gained acclaim for its innovative menu, impeccable service, and warm, inviting atmosphere. Ahmed's approach to cooking is deeply rooted in the use of fresh, locally sourced ingredients, combined with a creative flair that brings out the best in every dish. His dedication to excellence has made WESHNNE a beloved destination for food enthusiasts and a testament to his culinary expertise.

AboutWESHNNE

Welcome to WESHNNE, a culinary oasis located in the vibrant heart of Cairo. Founded by the renowned chef Ahmed Fawzy, WESHNNE is dedicated to offering a unique dining experience that blends traditional Egyptian flavors with modern culinary techniques.

Our story begins with Ahmed's passion for cooking, inspired by his family's rich culinary heritage. After mastering his craft at the prestigious Le Cordon Bleu in Paris and working alongside some of the world's most celebrated chefs, Ahmed returned to Cairo with a vision. In 2015, he brought that vision to life with the opening of WESHNNE.

At WESHNNE, we believe in using only the freshest, locally sourced ingredients to create dishes that are as flavorful as they are memorable. Our menu features a variety of innovative dishes that celebrate the best of Egyptian cuisine, all served in a warm and inviting atmosphere.

Join us at WESHNNE for an unforgettable culinary journey that honors tradition while embracing innovation. We look forward to serving you and making your dining experience truly special.
